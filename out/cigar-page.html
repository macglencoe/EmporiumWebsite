The ideal smoke for a seasoned palate, the Oliva Serie V Churchill Extra is a top-rated classic beloved by aficionados across the country. It is made at the Oliva familys TABOLISA factory in Nicaragua into a stocky 7x52 size and features choice Jalapa Valley Ligero tobaccos as the long fillers and binder. A top-quality Ecuadorian Sun Grown Habano wrapper completes the blend, and every leaf has been aged to perfection. The result is an incredibly rich and flavorful yet exceptionally smooth medium-full bodied smoke that provides complex flavors of coffee, peppery spice, cocoa, wood, leather, and tobacco sweetness for hours at a time.
